Welcome to Kadir Has University Clubs

Explore the diverse range of clubs at Kadir Has University, each with its unique purpose and vision. Connect with fellow students and discover exciting opportunities beyond traditional club promotion weeks.

Kadir Has University Clubs

Activism Club

The Kadir Has University Activism Club raises awareness about social, political, economic, and environmental changes through various platforms, including podcasts, debates, and cultural events. It advocates for rights and freedoms across various fields, fostering critical thinking and contributing to the development of conscious activists in the future.

Ataturkist Youth Club

The Kadir Has University Activism Club raises awareness about social, political, economic, and environmental changes through various platforms, including podcasts, debates, and cultural events. It advocates for rights and freedoms across various fields, fostering critical thinking and contributing to the development of conscious activists in the future.

Environment and Zero Waste Club

In order to increase awareness of environmental issues, Kadir Has University Environment and Zero Waste Club hosts various seminars and workshops with our members on topics such as the environment, climate crisis, sustainability, sustainable fashion, plant-based nutrition, and zero waste living.

Gender Identity and Sexual Orientation Studies Club

This club dedicated to fostering inclusivity, awareness, and understanding of gender identity and sexual orientation. Their purpose is to create a safe space for open dialogue, education, and support for individuals of all gender identities and sexual orientations.

cooKhas Club

CooKhas organizes seminars, in-person and online interviews, culinary events, excursions, and restaurant visits with the goal of increasing awareness in the field of gastronomy.

Mountain Climbing and Outdoor Sports Club

Rock climbing and winter mountaineering (Alpinism) are the interests of the Kadir Has University Mountaineering and Outdoor Sports Club (KHASDAK), a student organization. Club membership is open to anybody without prior experience or knowledge of extreme sports. New members will get theoretical and practical instruction from more seasoned club members, providing them with the information and expertise needed to partake in climbing activities.

Ethics and Philosophy Club

Because of the concept that underpins all of these, it provides a distinctive experience for anyone looking to enhance their capacity for critical thinking and logical inference, as well as to broaden their scope of thinking. We thus cordially encourage all students to join the club if they would want to partake in enjoyable and thought-provoking events while also making more deliberate contributions to society and their own lives.

Feminist Studies Club

The goal of the feminist club on campus is to educate, inform, and assist students on gender issues, women's rights, and gender equality. Our group wants to make the community and its members more aware of gender-related issues.

Entrepreneur Innovative Leaders Club

Not just in Istanbul but all around Turkey, the activities this club, which operates on a volunteer and personal basis, organizes, are gaining popularity. Since its founding, this group has only become better and has encouraged other colleges to start entrepreneurial clubs.

Google Developer Student Club

The Google Developer Student Club seeks to foster a community among college students interested in technology, equip them with technological know-how, and give them a venue on which to work on projects. The club's objective is to give students technical know-how and hands-on experience using Google resources.

HasGen Genetics and Science Club

Established in 2018, HASGEN (Science and Genetics organization) is a social and intellectual student organization with the goals of promoting science, enhancing industry-university collaboration, and hosting lectures, events, and meetings in a variety of scientific fields, particularly genetics. All of Kadir Has University's highly motivated science-interested students come together under one roof for HASGEN, an initiative designed to bring academics together.

Hashaspati

Hashaspati serves as a go-between, bringing in needing cats and dogs to a veterinarian. As a result, Hashaspati is not a facility that caters to university students' dogs and cats. We operate these organizations through sales of our merchandise (bags, bookmarks, badges, calendars, etc.) and donations from our members and/or animal lovers who want to assist us. Furthermore, one or more of our members may provide our kitties a temporary or permanent home.

Aviation Club

The goal of Aviation Club is to help members advance their careers by giving them a solid foundation in the aviation sector. The group works to build a community among aviation enthusiasts, give students access to industry developments, and equip them with information and skills related to aviation.

Law Club

The purpose of the Kadir Has University Law Society is to provide students with the opportunity to grow and change during their academic careers, as well as to enhance the knowledge they get in the department by participating in events. The Law Society seeks to develop not only competent and experienced legal practitioners but also people who are highly accountable, capable of critical thinking and problem-solving, with strong social and communication skills, articulate in speech, and adaptable in many domains.

Law and Economy Club

Law and Economy Club brings together students interested in law and economics with the goal of creating a platform that integrates these two fields. By tackling these two crucial subjects in an integrated way, the club hopes to educate its members on law and economics, equip them to monitor industry advancements, and present a variety of viewpoints.

Human Rights and Philosophy Club

The Kadir Has University Human Rights and Philosophy Club aims to raise awareness and ensure sustainability in human rights, discussing access, global and local issues, and individual or collective steps. It facilitates school-based awareness projects, focuses on application, and follows up on violations. The club aims to establish dialogues with students against discrimination, human rights violations, and violence.

International Student Club

Our goal is to include foreign and exchange students in Turkish society and university activities. We establish environments where students from Turkey and other countries may interact and get to know one another.We encourage our members' active participation, self-realization, and personal growth in a creative setting with an approachable and welcoming attitude. By doing this, we promote global friendship, understanding, and collaboration between all nations.

Business and Finance Club

Students interested in the business world and finance come together as part of the Business and Finance Club, which attempts to build a platform that unites these two crucial subjects. The club's objectives are to provide members with knowledge about business and finance, allow them to stay up to date on industry trends, and facilitate the establishment of professional networks.

Book Club

The goal of Book Club is to promote intellectual growth among readers by uniting people who enjoy a variety of genres and encouraging candid conversations from a range of viewpoints. Every month, members engage in a survey to choose the books, and at the conclusion of the month, conversations about the books are held. Through hosting exciting events and having pleasant discussions about writers, the club gains members who are eager to explore the vast world of literature.

Latin Dances Club

HasDans is a student club at a university since 2007, aiming to provide a platform for socialization and learning Latin dances. The club trains new members and participates in university dancing festivals and competitions. The club also hosts the Khas Dancing Festival, which hosts teams from various universities and professional dancers. Members receive training from the best dancers through workshops during the festival. The club aims to keep members confident and socially active.

Architecture Club

The Architecture Club at Kadir Has University aims to unite design students and interested individuals through events and talks, fostering a network for sharing and communication within the university's architecture field.

Model United Nations Club (HASMUN)

The Model United Nations Club, with 200 members, has been at Kadir Has University since 2009. It organizes conferences where participants discuss crises and offer solutions. The club has achieved sustainable success since 2014, hosting conferences like HASTRAIN, HASADVENCED, and HASMUN. The club organizes weekly events to improve English skills, foster discussion culture, and increase knowledge and self-awareness among high school and university students.

Engineering Club

Our club was founded in the academic year 2021–2022, and we are excited to welcome students from every department. The purpose of the club is to organize various seminars and project competitions related to the engineering department, as well as to receive updates on what is happening at the engineering departments.

Debate Club

The Kadir Has University Debate Club, established in 2022, aims to reintroduce debating culture to the university by collaborating with instructors and providing members with diction and public speaking skills. The club organizes debate events, tournaments, and inter-school debates, allowing speakers to share knowledge and experiences, and organizes Youth Workshops in areas like Art and Culture, Science and Technology, Human Rights, Popular Culture, and Entrepreneurship.

Music Club

Music is an important branch of arts that allows for emotional and thought transfer, improving relationships and fostering connections. The Kadir Has University Music Club, composed of eight friends, aims to maintain and promote music at the university. They organize events, interviews with artists, and create platforms for sharing experiences. The club believes that concerts by music groups will increase interest in music, making the university a social organization where emotions and thoughts are shared. Join the club to learn and share music in a great environment.

Psychology Club

In 2015, the psychology department's volunteer students created the Kadir Has Psychology Club. Anyone interested in psychology is welcome to join the group, not just psychology students. The Kadir Has Psychology Club meets with psychologists throughout the year and provides presentations and seminars on a range of subjects. It plans a variety of events, including entertaining school-wide parties, game evenings, book analyses, and movie screenings.

Art Club

Our art club is a community of artists and enthusiasts who celebrate creativity and express themselves through various mediums. We aim to create an inclusive space for everyone to explore their artistic potential. We host meetings, workshops, and events to share work, learn new techniques, and collaborate. Our main goal is to foster a culture of creativity and experimentation, encouraging members to explore different styles and perspectives. We also serve as a hub for art-related activities on campus, promoting community building and change.

Political Science and International Relations Club

The Political Science and International Relations Club at Kadir Has University aims to improve democracy by organizing events, tours, and workshops. It brings together students and politicians, promoting intellectual discussion skills and addressing all political agents equally. The club works in harmony with university stakeholders.

STUDIOHAS - Interior Architecture Club

StudioHas is an interior design-focused community founded in 2022. It aims to foster social and personal development among students in various design disciplines. The club organizes workshops, seminars, trips, and projects, allowing members to explore design principles, creative processes, and technical skills. Members can also showcase their projects and engage in design-related discussions.

Theater Club

The Theater Club at Kadir Has University, established in 2003, aims to promote and enhance theater interests at the university. As a university theater company, all events are innovative, research-based, and creative. The club has a free and democratic structure, with members directly participating in management. It comprises Dramaturgy, Technical Units, Stage Direction, Promotion and Organization, and Training units. The club emphasizes training to ensure knowledge and scientific background in its activities. The purpose is not just to stage plays annually, but to personal improvement among its members.

Sailing Club

The 2013 Sailing Club at Kadir Has University aims to unite seal enthusiasts and preserve marine culture. Whether you're a professional sailor, a team member, or interested in social events, the club welcomes everyone, eager to meet you.

Photography Club

Through workshops, our organization seeks to improve the technical credentials of photographers by offering chances for practice and theoretical understanding. Our goal is to form a community of learners who support one another and enjoy themselves in the process. Thanks to technology, anyone with a camera may join our group. To share our love of photography, we host events including expert photography talks, photography excursions, and exhibits around Istanbul.
